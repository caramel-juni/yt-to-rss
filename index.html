how do I get my youtube subscriptions file?
  1. go to takeout.google.com and sign in.
  2. click deselect all, then check only YouTube and YouTube Music.
  3. click All YouTube data included, deselect everything except subscriptions.
  4. export as a ZIP. wait a few mins for google to email you. or, just refresh the page after a coffee.
  5. unzip it, find subscriptions.csv, then drop that file below.

you could also just paste a list of channel IDs or channel URLs into the text box instead... but that's a lot of effort :p
